CSVV Project

COVID19 Student Vaccine Verification AI Based (CSVV)

The COVID19 Student Vaccine Verification System– AI-Based (CSVV), created to facilitate the Ministry of education save return to school plan, it will make it easier for schools to arrange entrance and keep students safe. Due to the increasing number of schools requests it became necessary to include all these problems in the platform that facilitates the student's entrance process for schools. Will be hard to check vaccine status and take attendance for all students. We facilitated this process so that all students will check their status and take their attendance. This system will help save time and effort for all schools. The primary goal of this report is to clarify all the functional and non-functional requirements, as well as the analysis and design models that should be used during the implementation phase. This project will be submitted as a final project for our Information Technology concentration major in the bachelor’s degree program at the Saudi Electronic University's College of Computing and Informatics.

What is CSVV Project?

CSVV ensure that no students allowed to access school without satisfy specific medical constrains as per decided by ministry of education to save students from COVID infection. To achieve that objective in fast and smart way we decided to develop this project to authorize the students who is following these constrains and denied who is not. Different methods and functions will be included to be helpful such as (Face recognition, skeleton based action) and additional features such as (Attendance tracking, notification message and diverse of interfaces with different authorization).
